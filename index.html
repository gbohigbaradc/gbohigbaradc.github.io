Featured Project: EcoWatch – AI-Powered Environmental Monitoring for Africa

Transforming Environmental Stewardship Across Nigeria and Africa

EcoWatch is an innovative, community-driven platform that leverages artificial intelligence to revolutionize environmental monitoring throughout Nigeria and Africa. Designed for oil-producing regions and beyond, EcoWatch empowers citizens, local field teams, and organizations to detect, report, and visualize environmental hazards in real time—driving actionable insights for the energy sector and policymakers.

  • AI-Powered Analysis: Users upload photos of water, land, or vegetation; the app instantly detects clean water, oil spills, or ecological damage using advanced machine learning.
  • Nationwide & Continental Reach: Scalable across Nigeria and Africa, EcoWatch brings vital environmental intelligence to communities from the Niger Delta to other critical ecosystems.
  • Live Mapping & Transparency: Geo-tagged reports build a dynamic risk map, enabling companies, NGOs, and government agencies to respond quickly and transparently to incidents.
  • Empowering Communities: Citizens become environmental guardians, providing ground-level data essential for compliance, ESG reporting, and sustainable development.
  • Strategic Value for Oil & Gas Leaders: EcoWatch bridges the gap between field realities and executive decisions, helping companies meet regulatory standards, mitigate risks, and enhance their reputation for responsible operations.
Why EcoWatch?
Environmental challenges in Africa’s oil and gas regions demand real-time monitoring and community engagement. EcoWatch turns every citizen into a data contributor, supporting sustainability and compliance at scale.
